Social Feed
Audio clips, waveform playback, and timestamp comments from musicians you follow

Discover

Everything You Need

Built differently, for musicians

01

Audio in the Feed

Upload practice clips and share them with a real waveform player. Listeners can comment at specific timestamps — turning feedback into a conversation tied to the music itself.

02

Timestamp Comments

When commenting on an audio post, your comment is pinned to the current playback position. Clicking a timestamp jumps anyone listening right to that moment in the recording.

03

Repertoire Management

Build your personal catalogue of pieces. Track what you are Learning, Practicing, and have Mastered — with difficulty levels and logged hours per piece.

04

Practice Statistics

Calendar heatmaps, weekly bar charts, and time-per-piece breakdowns. See your dedication visualized across days, weeks, and months.

05

Daily Streaks

Practice every day to build your streak. Earn Streak Freezes as a safety net. Milestones unlock badges that live permanently on your profile.

06

Discover by Instrument

Find pianists, violinists, guitarists, or any musician learning what you love. Follow them and their recordings appear in your feed automatically.

Who It Is For

Built for musicians in progress

Not just performers. Not just pros. Anyone learning, growing, and showing up.

Students

High school and college students learning classical instruments who want to share recordings and connect with others who understand the grind.

Hobby Musicians

Adult learners who play for the love of it and want to share recordings without the pressure of going viral or building a following.

Beginners

Anyone just starting out who wants a supportive place to post their first clips and get genuine feedback from more experienced players.

Our Philosophy

Not another highlight reel

Progress over Perfection

Share the struggle

Rough takes welcome. In-progress clips, not just polished recital recordings. The messy middle is where real growth happens and where Repertoire lives.

Consistency over Intensity

Small steps compound

Daily practice beats weekend marathons. Our streak system rewards showing up — even a 20-minute session counts.

Community over Competition

Musicians helping musicians

Timestamp feedback, celebrating milestones, answering technique questions — that is the kind of community Repertoire is designed to foster.

Authenticity over Curation

Real practice, real growth

No influencers. No algorithms optimizing for virality. Just musicians, their recordings, and genuine progress — day after day.
